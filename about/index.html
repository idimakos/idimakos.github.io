About Me...!

I work as an Assistant Professor of School Psychology at the University of Patras, Department of Education & Social Work. My research interests include the provision of psychological and counselling services in the school community, the identification and management of behavior problems in the schools and the investigation of writing skills especially through the usage of keystroke logging techniques.
